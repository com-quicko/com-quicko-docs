## Why Quicko Tools?

We've recognized the growing interest from fintech platforms looking to enhance their offerings with tax-related utilities. That's why we've developed themeable, plug & play tools that anyone can embed on their website or integrate into their mobile app.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ready-to-Use" icon="check-circle" defaultOpen>
    Pre-built tools that work out of the box. No need to build complex tax calculations or integrations from scratch.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Themeable" icon="palette" defaultOpen>
    Customize the look and feel to match your brand. Support for dark and light modes with Material 3 color system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Plug & Play" icon="plug">
    Simple integration via iframe for web or lightweight SDK for mobile apps. Get up and running in minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Always Updated" icon="refresh-cw">
    Tools are maintained and updated by Quicko to reflect the latest tax rules and regulations in India.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a Tool">
    Browse our [directory](/projects/tools/directory/index) to find the tools that fit your platform's needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick Your Channel">
    Decide whether you're integrating on [web](/projects/tools/integration/web/index) (iframe) or [mobile](/projects/tools/integration/mobile/index) (native SDK).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Configure [options](/projects/tools/options/index) to match your brand's theme and colors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Embed the tool and go live. Your users get instant access to powerful tax utilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>
