The Mobile SDK is an out-of-box solution tailored specifically for platforms looking to solve taxes for their users. The UI is customizable to the extent that users experience a fluid transition, without abrupt design breaks, providing them with a truly cohesive and unified user interface. It also comes equipped with robust connectors to our API, which, in turn, unlocks a plethora of tax-related functionalities.
